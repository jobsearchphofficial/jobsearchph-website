FAQ

Common questions

The essentials before you hire or apply.

How much does it cost?
For employers, our service fee is 20% of the candidate's agreed first-month salary, charged only when you successfully hire. No placement, no fee. Jobseekers are never charged anything.
When do I pay the fee?
The fee is due at turnover of the candidate's complete requirements, or the worker's first day — whichever comes first. Nothing is due upfront, and nothing is owed unless you actually hire.
Is it really free for jobseekers?
Yes. Workers never pay us, consistent with the Domestic Workers Act (RA 10361). Only employers are charged, and only after a successful hire.
What if the hire doesn't work out?
We offer a 30-day replacement guarantee. If a hired candidate leaves or proves unsuitable within 30 days of their first day, we provide one replacement at no additional service fee — subject to our terms.
How are candidates screened?
We conduct basic screening and verify candidate details and references before endorsing anyone. You always conduct the final interview and make the hiring decision yourself.
What areas do you serve?
Currently Bacolod, Talisay, Silay, and Bago City in Negros Occidental. We're focused on serving these areas well before expanding.
How do I get started?
Employers: send your job request by email or Messenger. Jobseekers: apply through our application form. We'll review and take it from there — see the options below.
